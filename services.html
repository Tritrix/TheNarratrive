Coaching

Stories are an intrinsic part of our lives. We are our stories – the stories we share with others and the deeply personal stories we tell ourselves. Yet, we fail to recognize its potential in helping us get in touch with our true self, forge lasting relationships, and in making life more purposeful.

Storytelling is at the heart of all that is offered at The Narrative. Through our coaching process, which is strictly a one-on-one affair, we will help you find the stories that define you – stories of your aspirations, beliefs and values. In the course of our time together, you will learn the essentials to keep in mind while drafting the story of your future; you will also discover the tools to live that story. A deeply transformative experience, it always succeeds in leaving a lasting impression – as much on you as on others around you.

Storytelling

Whether you are seeking to influence your audience to your perspective, pitch an idea to a potential group of investors, motivate your team to meet targets, or, simply just leave a lasting impression, it is ALL in the way you present. It is as much about the structure of your presentation as it is about the way you deliver it. It is about engaging with your audience.

Stories not only help you establish a better connect with your audience, they also aid in getting the message across to your listeners, effectively. So that, they respond favorably.

The Narrative offers highly personal and customized training to help you present more effectively, be it to a group or an individual; be it at work or in social settings. While training with The Narrative to hone your presentation skills, you will learn ways to use stories to make your presentation more engaging and compelling.

Content

People are more swayed by emotions than by logic because emotions help make that desired connection with the audience. This is precisely why stories are more effective than mere facts and statistics in engaging with an audience.

Applying the principles of storytelling helps greatly in creating content that appeals. Be it an idea, product or service that you want to sell, a message that you want to share, or information you want people to be aware of, The Narrative can help you strike the right chord with your readers. Websites, brochures, coffee table books, speeches, stories – just to give you a sample of the possibilities
