About

Indu Divya

Every moment holds the promise to change the course of our life. One such instant in my life was when I watched a storyteller render a compelling story with a profound message. It instantly hooked me – to the power of a story and the art of storytelling. Ever since, stories have become an integral part of my life – and communication. Attending workshops, staging performances and conducting workshops on storytelling have helped me hone my skills as an artist and explore various opportunities where I could use them.

More recently, that episode helped me craft my own story. A story of discovering my purpose, of overcoming my fears and of taking a leap of faith. It is called The Narrative.

My career so far, has been an enriching journey across fields as diverse as Public Relations, Learning & Development, Storytelling and Content Writing. However, the primary focus has always been on communicating and connecting, on being real, on creating an impact. The Narrative builds on this cumulative experience to offer clients services in coaching, to help them find and draft their own story for the future; training, to help them present with power and purpose; and, developing content that is both engaging and compelling.
