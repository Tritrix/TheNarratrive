Vision

The Narrative was founded with the vision to help people communicate effectively and connect better – with their own selves and with others – by harnessing the power of stories.

Mission

The Narrative aims at helping people engage effectively – with their own selves and others – through its slew of offerings that include story based coaching, training and content development. The Narrative hosts workshops besides conducting and participating in storytelling events.

Coaching is a strictly one-to-one service to help Clients discover and draft their stories for the future.

Training helps participants become persuasive presenters so that they may present with power and purpose. It is done for a group of people. Content development employs the elements of storytelling to create and develop content that is both, engaging and interesting for Clients.
